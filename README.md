🐾 Street Pawtection — Rescue Begins With You

A social impact web application that allows users to report and help rescue injured or hungry street animals through geolocation, images, and descriptions. The goal is to connect the public with rescue teams and adoption organizations using technology for good.

🐾 **Key Features**

1. **Report a Street Animal**
**Location Input:** Users can enter the location of the animal in need.
**Image Upload:** Allows uploading a photo of the animal for better identification.
**Description Field:** Users can provide details about the animal's condition, such as injuries or signs of starvation.
**Submit Report:** A button to submit the report, which would ideally send the information to rescue teams.

2. **Helpline Support**
**24/7 Helpline Information:** Displays a contact number for users who may not have internet access, allowing them to report animals via call or SMS.

3. **Volunteer & Adoption Opportunities**
**Volunteer Sign-Up:** Encourages users to join as volunteers to assist in rescue operations or foster care.
**Adoption Listings:** Provides information on animals available for adoption, helping them find permanent homes.

4. **User-Friendly Interface**
**Responsive Design:** The website is accessible on various devices, including smartphones and tablets.
**Clean Layout:** Information is organized in a straightforward manner, making it easy for users to navigate.
